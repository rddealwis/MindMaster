Mind Master is a very simple game where you have to memorize the sequence of colored tiles. 

The aim of the game is to find the correct order which the tiles got colored.

The game difficulty increases with the level.

There are 3 game modes:

~ Easy ~
Quite easy but be patient.

~ Medium ~
You have less than 1 second to memorize the tile sequence.

~ Hard ~
It will drive you crazy!


~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Be the best on each mode & test how good your short term memory is.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
